Our Story

Lumeza began with inspiration drawn from two craft capitals of India. Saharanpur, known for its timeless woodwork, brings tradition, skill, and natural warmth. Moradabad, celebrated globally for its metal craftsmanship, adds strength, precision, and modern character. By combining these two worlds, Lumeza creates products that feel intentional, functional, and enduring—designs made to elevate everyday living while honoring the craft legacy of both cities.

Why Choose Lumeza?

Dual-craft heritage

Genuine woodwork from Saharanpur paired with precision metalwork from Moradabad.

Minimal luxury

Modern, durable designs that read premium without needless ornament.

Ethical small-batch

Limited runs, careful finishing, and attention to materials.
